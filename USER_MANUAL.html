1. Introduction

Welcome to the Juan365 Creative Ticketing System - a comprehensive platform designed to streamline creative request management, approval workflows, and team collaboration.

1.1 Purpose

This system enables:

  • Submitting creative requests (social media posts, banners, videos, ads)
  • Two-step approval workflow (Department Manager â†’ Creative Manager)
  • Task assignment and progress tracking
  • Real-time notifications via Telegram
  • Performance analytics and reporting

1.2 System Requirements

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Internet connection
  • Valid user account (approved by administrator)

1.3 Accessing the System

System URL: https://juan365-ticketing-frontend.vercel.app

2. Getting Started

2.1 Creating an Account

1 Go to the Registration Page

Click "Register" on the login page or navigate to /register

2 Fill in Your Information
  • Email: Your work email address
  • Username: Unique username for login
  • First Name / Last Name: Your full name
  • Password: Minimum 8 characters
  • Department: Select your department
3 Wait for Approval

Your account must be approved by an administrator before you can log in.

Note: After 3 failed login attempts, your account will be locked for 30 minutes.

2.2 Logging In

  1. Enter your email or username
  2. Enter your password
  3. Click "Sign In"

2.3 Forgot Password

  1. Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
  2. Enter your registered email
  3. Check your email for reset link (valid for 24 hours)
  4. Create a new password

3. User Roles & Permissions

The system has three user roles with different access levels:

Member

Regular team members who create and track tickets.

  • Create tickets for their department
  • View and track their own tickets
  • Add comments and attachments
  • Confirm completed work

Manager

Department managers who approve tickets and manage their team.

  • All Member permissions
  • Approve/reject department tickets
  • Assign tasks to Creative team
  • View team analytics
  • Manage department users

Admin

System administrators with full access.

  • All Manager permissions
  • Manage all users and departments
  • Create/edit products
  • Access all tickets across departments
  • Permanently delete tickets

5. Creating Tickets

5.1 Starting a New Ticket

  1. Click "+ Create New Ticket" button
  2. Fill in the required information
  3. Add attachments if needed
  4. Click "Submit Ticket"

5.2 Ticket Fields

Field Required Description
Title Yes Brief description of the request
Description Yes Detailed requirements and specifications
Department Yes Target department (auto-filled for non-admins)
Priority Yes Urgent, High, Medium, or Low

6.2 Status Descriptions

Status Badge What Happens Next
For Dept Approval For Dept Approval Department manager reviews and approves
For Creative Approval For Creative Approval Creative manager reviews and approves
Not Yet Started Not Yet Started Assigned designer clicks "Start Editing"
In Progress In Progress Designer works and clicks "Complete"
Completed Completed Requester confirms or requests revision
Rejected Rejected Ticket closed - no further action

6.3 Two-Step Approval

Step 1 - Department Approval: Your department manager reviews the request to ensure it's valid and within scope.
Step 2 - Creative Approval: The Creative team manager reviews the request for feasibility and assigns resources.
Tip: Creative team members' tickets skip Step 1 and go directly to Creative Approval.

7. Managing Tickets

7.1 Viewing Tickets

Navigate to Tickets in the sidebar to see all tickets you have access to.

Filtering Options:

  • Status: Filter by current status
  • Priority: Filter by urgency level
  • Search: Search by title or description
  • Date Range: Filter by creation date

View Modes:

  • Table View: Traditional list format
  • Card View: Visual card layout

7.2 Ticket Details

Click on any ticket to view its full details including:

  • Complete description and requirements
  • Attachments and files
  • Comments and discussion
  • Activity history
  • Available actions

7.3 Adding Comments

  1. Open the ticket details
  2. Scroll to the Comments section
  3. Type your message
  4. Click "Add Comment"
Tip: Use comments to ask questions, provide updates, or give feedback.

7.4 Uploading Attachments

  1. Open the ticket details
  2. Click "Add Attachment" or drag files to the upload area
  3. Supported formats: Images, Videos, PDFs, Documents

7.5 Available Actions by Role

Action Who Can Do It When Available
Approve (Dept) Department Manager Status: Requested
Approve (Creative) Creative Manager/Admin Status: Pending Creative
Reject Managers/Admin Before completion
Assign Creative Manager/Admin After creative approval
Start Editing Assigned Designer Status: Approved (Not Yet Started)
Complete Assigned Designer Status: In Progress
Confirm Requester Status: Completed
Request Revision Requester Status: Completed
Rollback Managers From History tab

7.6 Requesting Revisions

If the completed work needs changes:

  1. Open the completed ticket
  2. Click "Request Revision"
  3. Explain what needs to be changed
  4. Submit - ticket returns to "In Progress"
Note: Revision count is tracked for analytics.

11. Troubleshooting

11.1 Common Issues

Can't log in?

  • Check if your account is approved
  • Verify email/username and password
  • Account may be locked after 3 failed attempts (wait 30 minutes)
  • Use "Forgot Password" to reset

Can't see my tickets?

  • Check your filter settings
  • Clear the search box
  • You may only see tickets related to your department

Can't approve/assign tickets?

  • Verify you have the correct role (Manager/Admin)
  • Check if ticket is in the correct status
  • Only Creative department managers can assign

Attachments not uploading?

  • Check file size (max 10MB recommended)
  • Verify file format is supported
  • Try a different browser

11.2 Getting Help

  • Account issues
